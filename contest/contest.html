Rules & Regulations
These rules are for all contests hold by CodeFest.pk only. To checkout rules for specific contest Please go to its specific page by clicking START NOW button at the bottom of specific contest card.
Each contest by CodeFest is a remote contest with some registration fee which you have to pay while submiting the project.
This registration fee is used by us to reward the winners & for paying other dues.
We will never make profit from the registration fees because it will be haram for us.
Each contest will have some specific time limit.
You can participate as many contests as you want.
Only two to three participants will get refund as per contest.
There will be no refund, after submission of projects.
Winners will be judged according to neatness of code, & as per requirements of specific contest.
We will decide winners after the contest has ended & announce the results on our Website & on our Instagram.
We will talk to the winners through Instagram or Whatsapp to give them their rewards.
Follow our Instagram to not miss any updates about contests, & about other things like, Terms, Privacy Policy, Rules etc.
Please make sure you have read all requirements & rules of specific contest before participating.
Your payments will be completely secured by specific payment gateway.
The upcoming contests will be decided by the founder. You can also give ideas for codefests on Instagram or on our Whatsapp.
You can use all the technologies mentioned in the requirements of specific contest. You can also reuse the code, & try to not copy/paste code of or from other resources. So your skills can be improved.
We will make sure that everyone will get equal rights & there will be no place for cheaters.
If you have any queries, Please feel free to a contact us through Instagram or through our Whatsapp.

Codefest is a platform for beginner and professional developers. We hold online programming contests for developers. Founded in 2023 by a teenager programmer named as Hadi Naseer.
