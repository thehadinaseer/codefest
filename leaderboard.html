How to get points to rank high?
Only 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th & 5th contest winners will get points.
Each contest winner will get some points & in leaderboard he/she will be ranked according to his/her points.
Leader Board will be updated every time the contest winners are announced.
With the reward the winners will also get points.
1st one will get 1000 points.
2nd one will get 500 points.
3rd one will get 350 points.
4th one will get 150 points.
5th one will get 80 points.
